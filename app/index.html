About

Hi, I'm Lars

I am a Front-End Developer & UX/UI Designer. I love solving problems; design problems, coding problems, usability problems, and I'm not afraid to get my hands dirty doing so. I also enjoy learning new things and improving my skills so I'm constantly learning. Luckily there's never a shortage of things to learn when it comes to front-end development #JuniorDevForLife.

I studied design and usability, however I quickly started learning how to code so that I could be more self-sufficient and bring my designs to fruition.

About me

I was born in Norway before the internet even existed. I got my first computer when I was 11, a Commdore 64 and after that an Amiga 500 before getting my first PC at age 15, an Intel 486DX/33. Ever since then, I knew I wanted to work with computers in one way or another. Throughout my life I've lived in Dar Es Salam, Maputo, Trondheim, Brisbane and London before settling down in Melbourne for better part of the last decade.

I am a husband, a father, a gamer and a computer geek...
